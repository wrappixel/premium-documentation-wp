Support

We at WrapPixel.com are here to assist with support for our templates. If you need help, please submit a ticket at :- wrappixel.com/support
We kindly ask that before submitting a ticket, you take a moment to review the full documentation. This will help ensure your question is related to support and that the issue you're facing hasn’t already been addressed. By doing so, we can provide you with a quicker and more accurate response

Covered Services

  • Addressing questions or concerns related to the template
  • Resolving any reported bugs.

What’s Not Covered

  • Installation services are not included
  • Support for third-party plugins or software is not offered
  • Assistance with integrating technologies such as PHP, .NET, Java, etc., is not available
  • We do not resolve issues within customized or implemented templates

Custom Work

We at WrapPixel.com are here to help with any custom work you may need. If you require tailored solutions or specific adjustments, please submit a request at: wrappixel.com/hire-us
